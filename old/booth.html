Booth (NEW!!)

Traditionally, JSA's major annual event Matsuri is held every spring. This year, the exec board has decided to try something a little different this year: Matsuri during the fall semester, and a carnival booth during the spring semester!

Although Spring Carnival is still a way's away, we wanted to spread the word first. We welcome all JSA members to join in, and even non-members as well who just want to partake in a fun CMU tradition.

If you have any interest, please fill out this google form! Hope to see you at our first Booth GBM.
